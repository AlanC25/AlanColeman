I love music! I could also say I breath air and eat food, because doesn't everyone ? That being said, I also have played guitar for about 10 years, and dabble in a few other instruments such as keyboard and harmonica.

<!-- excerpt end -->

## Playing Live

As luck would have it, my girlfriend happens to be a classically trained Soprano. When she's not singing incredibly difficult classical pieces, she can often be found singing old pop songs accompanied by myself. Mostly, this has been for our own enjoyment in private, but we have played on couple of occasions for the local pub's music nights.

## G.A.S

A love for music is not all positives however, and during the first coronavirus lockdown, I found myself with bad GAS(Gear Aquisiton Syndrome, ask r/guitarpedals if you don't believe it's a real thing). It began with buying 1 guitar pedal, only to end up with 8 a few months later. Fortunately I have managed not to purchase any more in several months and a, delighted with my current setup.
