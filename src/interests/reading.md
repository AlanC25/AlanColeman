I have always been a big reader. Growing up I read all 13 books in the Series of Unfortunate Events. I also read a lot of Anthony Horrowitz, including the Alex Rider, Diamond Brothers and Power of 5 books.

<!-- excerpt end -->

## As I got older

Once I grew out of young adult books, it took me a while to find authors I really enjoyed. Books advertised on morning shows like Ireland AM, or advertised as the latest best-selling thriller didn't really seem aimed at me. It wasn't until I discovered writers such as Irvine Welsch, Kurt Vonnegut, Hunter S. Thompson, etc. that I found a foothold in what I wanted to be reading. 

## Lately

After reading Terry Pratchett & Neil Gaiman's fantasticly hilarious book 'Good Omens' last year, I have found myself reading a lot of Pratchett's brilliant Discworld books. Also other similar styled books such as the classic 'Hitch-hikers Guide to the Galaxy' series.
