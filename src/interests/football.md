Growing up I always enjoyed sport. While I'm a fan of GAA, Tennis and Rugby, it is english football (or "soccer", bleh) that has always interested me most.

<!-- excerpt end -->

Growing up, I played for my local club, Enfield Celtic, at underage level. When I moved to Kinnegad, I then played for Kinnegad Juniors.
## NDSL

The highlight of playing with Kinnegad Juniors came when we very unexpectedly got promoted to the Premier Division of the North Dublin SchoolboysLeague. It was a huge achievement for such a small town to do so as it is considered the best schoolboy league in the country.  

## More recently

I'm such a football fan, that I've even subjected myself to watching all of the Republic of Ireland's home games on a season ticket I have renewed over the last few years, I can confirm Christian Eriksen will likely haunt me forever. As I've gotten older I've made a point to make time for occasional games of 5-a-side with friends and colleagues. I can also be found most weekends watching Liverpool games and updating my fanatsy football team.
